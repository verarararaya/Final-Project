Welcome to bdicACP

Our website is designed to provide access for our peptide predictor, named as bdicACP Predictor.This predictor takes peptide sequences as input data,
and return with a number suggesting how confident it is sure that the peptides input can be classified as anti-cancer peptide or not.To build this predictor,
we trained models by Neural Network algorithm, unlike many existing predictor. We used peptide databases of ACP500 and ACP164, respectively for
training andtesting.We select the best performing models out of 11 models and use that model as our predictor. Benchmarking suggesting our model has
mcc around 0.6, which is higher than most predictors withsimilar function.
